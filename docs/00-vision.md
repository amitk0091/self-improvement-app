# 00 — Vision

## One-line vision
A personal operating system that makes self-improvement *frictionless and self-correcting* — log in 60 seconds, see patterns, get one weekly nudge that actually changes behavior.

## The real problem
Tracking tools fail not from lack of features but from **abandonment**. Prior attempts survived ~6–7 days. The differentiator here is ruthless reduction of friction + streak pressure + AI that reflects back what you can't see yourself.

## Principles
1. **Friction is the enemy.** Sub-60s daily log or it dies.
2. **Focus over coverage.** Two goals (SWE + Fitness) dominate. Other ambitions = tags, not modules.
3. **Reflect, don't just record.** Data is worthless without weekly insight.
4. **Honesty over vanity.** Streaks can break; the app helps recover, not shame.
5. **Build small, ship, use daily.** A tool you abandon = failure even if "complete".

## Success metric (12 months)
- 80%+ days logged for 3 straight months.
- Measurable SWE hours + workout consistency trend up.
- At least one acted-upon AI insight per month.

## Non-goals (v1)
- Multi-user / SaaS. - Full business/finance/SSC modules. - Mobile native app. - Social features.

## North star
You open it daily because it costs nothing and gives clarity. Everything else is secondary.
